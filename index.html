Summary

Diabetes in General: Why Should We Care?

In 2 years, the percentage of the total US population diagnosed with diabetes went from

9% to 10.5%



That's 32.4 million individuals in the US. Moreover, many people may be predibetic and completely unaware. It is estimated that 9 out of 10 Americans are prediabetic.

Diabetes is 7th leading cause of death in the US



The ADA report that more Americans die from diabetes every year than from AIDS and breast cancer combined. According to the CDC, 79,535 deaths occur each year due to diabetes. The number of fatalities related to diabetes may be underreported.
